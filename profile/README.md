# NOVA HEALTH 🇹🇬

## What is NOVA HEALTH?

NOVA HEALTH is a digital coordination platform built to strengthen Togo's national health system. It connects hospitals, clinics, and health centers so they can share critical information in real time  like bed availability, medical staff presence, and equipment status  ensuring patients get to the right facility faster.

## Why it matters

Togo's main national hospital, the CHU Sylvanus Olympio, is chronically overwhelmed. Patients often travel hours to reach it, even when local health centers have space and the right expertise. Meanwhile, doctors trying to transfer patients between facilities rely on phone calls that are slow, untracked, and often outdated.

NOVA HEALTH fixes this by giving the health system a shared nervous system: live visibility of resources across the network, and instant, traceable coordination for every patient transfer.

## What it does

**For patients:** A mobile app helps you describe your symptoms and guides you to the nearest available health facility that can treat you  before you even leave home. It respects the national care pyramid, so you are directed to local centers when appropriate, reserving major hospitals for serious cases.

**For doctors and nurses:** A secure web dashboard shows the entire health network in real time. You can see which facilities have beds, which specialists are available, and initiate a patient transfer with a single click. The receiving team is notified instantly, and every step is tracked.

**For hospital managers:** Supervisors update bed occupancy and staff presence in seconds using QR codes. The entire network sees the change immediately, preventing dangerous overcrowding and underuse.

**For the health system:** Every transfer is documented and traceable, creating the first national dataset on patient flows between facilities. This helps the Ministry of Health identify bottlenecks and improve care nationwide.

## Our commitment

• Patient data is protected and never shared without authorization  
• The platform supports offline and low-connectivity environments  
• All coordination is neutral  no facility is favored over another  
• Designed with doctors, for doctors, validated by Togo's medical community
